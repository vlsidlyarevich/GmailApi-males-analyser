Online sales analysis


Analysis of marketing market shows that online sales occupy a large part. Users need an accessible tool for the analysis of their own online shopping. Our solution allows you to analyze your email and bring cost graphics quickly and easily.

Google support


There is no need to worry about your personal information or email. Our application provides you official Google support. We don't store your personal information, but Google stores. Google provides us all needed information and all that you need is to sign in with Google.

Steam store support


Steam store includes over 3,500 games from Action to Indie and everything in-between. It offer a catalog of PC, Mac, and Linux games, many of which support Steam Play. It has the largest gaming community, that's why we support steam.

Origin support


The Origin store allows users to browse and purchase games for full price from Electronic Arts' catalogs. Instead of receiving a box, disc, or even CD key, purchased software is immediately attached to the user's Origin account and is to be downloaded with the corresponding Origin client.
